# Q3 Business Review

A small sample document for the axonmind-open quickstart. Indexing it with the
default rule extractor produces three KPI nodes plus the driver and blocker
relationships between them — no LLM or API key required.

# Customer Acquisition Cost

Customer Acquisition Cost measures how much we spend to win one new customer.

# Churn Rate

Churn Rate is the percentage of customers we lose each month.

# Revenue Growth

Revenue Growth tracks the month-over-month change in recurring revenue.

# How the metrics relate

Customer Acquisition Cost influences Revenue Growth across every segment.

Churn Rate blocks Revenue Growth whenever monthly retention falls.
