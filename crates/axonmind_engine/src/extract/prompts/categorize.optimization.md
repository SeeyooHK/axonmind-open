Quality checklist:
- Prefer 4-8 categories when possible; only go near 10 when the graph clearly supports it.
- Use distinct business themes. Avoid duplicate or near-duplicate category names.
- Keep "Other" as a single small catch-all. Use it only for true leftovers after thematic grouping.
- Choose headlines that are meaningful at a glance: prioritize KPI/Metric/Objective nodes when they represent the group.
- Ensure labels are clean and human-readable (no raw IDs, no clause numbers, no trailing punctuation).
- Use edge connectivity to keep closely related nodes in the same category when feasible.
